The Four Trines
Elemental Years — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water
Each zodiac year carries a heavenly stem element that cycles every 2 years. This gives each sign a deeper layer — a Wood Horse vs an Earth Horse are the same sign but different in temperament and drive.

Wood — Growth-oriented, flexible, creative, idealistic. Visionaries who build from seed.
Fire — Passionate, bold, charismatic, restless. Natural leaders who burn bright.
Earth — Grounded, reliable, patient, practical. The ones who make things real and lasting.
Metal — Disciplined, determined, precise, ambitious. They cut through noise and execute.
Water — Intuitive, diplomatic, fluid, perceptive. Readers of people and unseen currents.

Example: Joshua and Tyrese are both Water Horses — same sign, same element, born 2002. This is exceptionally rare and explains their deep resonance. Quincy Farley is an Earth Horse — grounded execution energy vs their fluid wisdom.
